Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2025

I believe in privacy by design. This website is a static portfolio and does not use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes.

1. Data Collection

I do not collect any personal data unless you voluntarily provide it via the contact form. The contact form uses Formsubmit.co to securely forward your message to my email. That data is not stored on any database I control.

2. Cookies

This site does not drop any persistence cookies. We use local storage only for essential UI preferences (like potential dark mode in the future).

3. Third-Party Services

We use Google Fonts and Tailwind CDN for styling. These services may collect IP addresses for request delivery, governed by their respective privacy policies.
